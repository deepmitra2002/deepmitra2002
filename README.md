# 💫 About Me:
**Deep Mitra**<br>
*CSE Student | Aspiring Cybersecurity Specialist | Full-Stack Learner*<br><br>
I am a Computer Science and Engineering student at Daffodil International University with a deep interest in deconstructing complex systems—from the inner workings of a compiler to the security of a network. My expertise lies at the intersection of academic rigor in Automata Theory and hands-on exploration in Ethical Hacking.<br><br>
Currently, I am building a strong foundation in penetration testing, network security, and secure software development. I enjoy working on projects that solve real-world problems, such as my SmartGate proxy server for traffic management or the Bangladesh Patients Records (BPR) system for secure medical data handling.<br><br>

**Technical Toolkit:**<br>
* **Languages:** Python, Java (OOP), C/C++, 8086 Assembly, SQL, Bash Scripting.<br>
* **Cybersecurity:** Kali Linux, Metasploit, Nmap, Wireshark, Burp Suite, Squid Proxy.<br>
* **Core CS:** Compiler Design, Operating Systems, Computer Networking (VLAN, OSPF, ACLs), Data Science.<br>
* **Tools:** Linux (Ubuntu/Debian), VMware, VirtualBox, Git, XAMPP.<br><br>

My goal is to become a skilled Cybersecurity Professional and Software Engineer, continuously learning through hands-on labs, CTF challenges, and AI/ML research. When I’m not in the terminal, I’m likely watching anime (Studio Ghibli and Demon Slayer) or advocating for digital privacy and security awareness.<br><br>
📍 Based in Dhaka, Bangladesh<br>
🎓 Studying at Daffodil International University<br>
